Duties

1. Construction of new wooden houses and reinforced steel-framed apartments, etc. Rapid water drainage, sanitation and air-conditioning system construction

We provide water supply equipment,installation of drainage facilities, hot water supply facilities, sanitary fixtures (washbasins, toilets), air conditioning facilities, ventilation facilities, etc.

2. Water renovation work

Remodeling of kitchens, bathrooms and other water-related areas (replacement of system kitchens, installation of underfloor storage, Replacement of unit bath, replacement of toilet seat, etc.)

3. Subject to the water tank straight knot chemical matter

This construction work involves removing the receiving water tanks used in buildings and condominiums and directly supplying clean and hygienic water. In addition to providing fresh, clean water at all times, the project also reduces the cost of maintenance and management of the receiving water tank.

4. Launch cut replacement works

We will switch over toilet tanks and septic tanks to public sewage system. Odors and other odors will be eliminated and the living sanitation environment will be improved.

5. Water supply work for residential land development

When building lots for housing and other residential development, we will install water supply and drainage pipes from public water pipes into the building.

6. Water and sewer construction works

This is a construction project to install new water supply and drainage pipes on a lot that currently has no water supply or drainage pipes.

7. Construction of rainwater infiltration facilities

This is a construction project that allows rainfall on roofs and other structures to percolate into the ground as quickly as possible. Currently, the Sewerage Bureau also recommends this construction. Hachioji City also offers subsidies and other programs.
